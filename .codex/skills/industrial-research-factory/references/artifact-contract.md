# Artifact Contract

The cycle is complete only when every path declared under `outputs` in
`protocol/CYCLE_SPEC.yaml` exists and is non-empty. The validation report must
record reference/Numba agreement, fixed-seed reproduction, replication outcome,
and uncertainty. The performance report must separate JIT warm-up from warmed
execution. Reports must say that the microcycle does not establish novelty or
publication-ready evidence.
