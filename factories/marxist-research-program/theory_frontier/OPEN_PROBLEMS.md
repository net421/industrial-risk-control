# Open Problems and Research Frontier

**Status:** Scope scaffold only. No cycle has yet produced validated findings.

This file records problems that remain open after main-line textual analysis, historical case evaluation, critique adjudication, and reconciliation mapping.

## Required Entry Format

Each open problem should include:

- problem id;
- topic;
- main-line position;
- historical pressure or unresolved contradiction;
- rival or comparative insights;
- evidence already reviewed;
- missing evidence;
- proposed theory-extension hypothesis;
- compatibility assessment with the main line;
- confidence level;
- next research action.

## Initial Research Frontiers To Scope Later

1. State, transition, and bureaucracy.
2. Socialist planning under complex modern production networks.
3. Imperialism, financialization, digital platforms, and global value chains.
4. Party, class organization, and mass initiative.
5. National liberation, dependent development, and socialist transition.
6. Revisionism, reformism, and the conditions under which parties and institutions change over time.
7. Prediction audit: which classical predictions held, failed, or require boundary conditions.
8. Reconciliation map: which disputes among revolutionary Marxists were fundamental and which were historically contingent.
