# Evaluation Schema Reference

The `evaluations/` directory contains CSV schemas that should be filled only after a research question has been frozen and sources have been audited.

## `HISTORICAL_CASES.csv`
Records real historical cases, observed outcomes, achievements, failures, and boundary conditions.

## `PROS_CONS_MATRIX.csv`
Separates achievements from failures or limitations for a case, author, doctrine, or implementation.

## `PREDICTION_AUDIT.csv`
Tracks predictions or expectations and classifies outcomes as confirmed, partially confirmed, contradicted, or unresolved.

## `INCONGRUENCE_REGISTER.csv`
Records contradictions, tensions, mismatches, or unresolved problems.

## `CRITIQUE_ADJUDICATION.csv`
Separates justified, unjustified, mixed, and unresolved critiques.

## `AGREEMENT_DISAGREEMENT_MATRIX.csv`
Maps where authors or traditions agree and disagree.

## `RECONCILIATION_MATRIX.csv`
Assesses whether disagreements are reconcilable, partially reconcilable, not reconcilable, or unresolved.

## Important

Template rows are not findings. Replace them only with evidence-linked rows.
